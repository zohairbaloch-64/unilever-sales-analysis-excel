# 📊 Unilever Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales analysis solution built in Microsoft Excel using one week of Unilever sales data. The objective was to analyze sales performance, product performance, and customer behavior through dynamic dashboards, KPI cards, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

The project is divided into three separate dashboards:

- Sales Performance Dashboard
- Product Performance Dashboard
- Customer Analysis Dashboard

---

##  Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing products and brands
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate sales distribution across channels and markets
- Create interactive dashboards for business decision-making

- Category
- Brand
- Market
- Sub Brand

- Sales Volume
- Sales Value

- Tax
- Net Amount
- Sales Cases
- Sales Pieces
- Invoice Number

#  Dashboard 1: Sales Performance Dashboard

### KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Total Products
- Total Quantity Sold

### Visualizations

- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Channel
- Revenue by Market
- Daily Sales Trend

### Purpose

Provides an overall view of sales performance and highlights revenue trends across different business dimensions.

---

#  Dashboard 2: Product Performance Dashboard

### KPIs

- Total Products
- Total Brands
- Top Product Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold

### Visualizations

- Top 10 Products
- Top 10 Brands
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Sub Brand

### Purpose

Analyzes product performance and identifies high-performing products and brands.

#  Dashboard 3: Customer Analysis Dashboard
